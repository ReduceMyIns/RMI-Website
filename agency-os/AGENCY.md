## Call Handling Rules

### ALWAYS Transfer (Full Service carriers)
General questions, coverage questions, policy changes, billing, claims — transfer is the fastest path.

### Transfer for Billing/Claims ONLY (Billing & Claim Service carriers)
- Due dates, grace periods, payment methods
- Filing a new claim
- Status of an existing claim

### NEVER Transfer — Handle Internally
- Policy cancellation requests
- Certificate of Insurance (COI) requests
- New quotes for policies the caller doesn't already have
- Re-shopping for a lower price
- Any complaint about the carrier, adjuster, inspector, or auditor

### New Vehicle Rule
If caller wants insurance for a new car: **determine if they have an existing active auto policy first.**
- If YES → policy CHANGE (add vehicle), not a new policy. Full Service carriers handle directly.
- If NO → new business quote → Chase.

### When Not to Transfer
Gather details, then book appointment with:
- **Chase**: New business, re-shopping, retention, IT issues, accounting
- **Sherry**: Policy changes, billing disputes, underwriting follow-ups, existing policy services
